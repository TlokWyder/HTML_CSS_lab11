Heritage of Anvar University

Why preserve the legacy?

Anvar University (AU) was founded in 2040 as a future‑oriented IT university. The campus, digital labs and online platforms were designed from day one for cloud, AI and cybersecurity education — this page keeps that story alive.

Key milestones of Anvar University

2040

Foundation of Anvar University

Dr. Anvar Beisembiyev establishes Anvar University, focused on practical IT, programming and digital transformation.

2045

First AU Campus & Online Platform

The main campus opens together with a powerful online platform so students can study from any region.

2050

AI & Cybersecurity Schools

New schools of Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity train specialists for international companies.

2058

Global Partnership Network

AU signs cooperation agreements with leading universities in Europe and Asia, launching joint degree programs.

2067

Alumni Innovation Fund

Graduates of Anvar University create a fund to support student startups in fintech, edtech and robotics.
